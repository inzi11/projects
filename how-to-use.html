How To Use Weather Forecast

Using Weather Forecast is easy and intuitive. Follow these simple steps to get the most out of our weather forecasting service:

  1. Start on the Home page to check the current weather for your location.
  2. Use the search bar to find weather information for a different location by entering a city or location name and clicking the "Search" button.
  3. Explore the detailed weather information, including temperature, humidity, wind speed, and more.
  4. Switch to the Weather Forecast page to plan your week with a 7-day weather forecast.
  5. View information about sunrise and sunset times to help you plan your activities.
  6. For historical weather data, check the Last 24 Hours Weather page.

Weather Forecast is designed to make it easy for you to access accurate and up-to-date weather information for any location. Enjoy your weather forecasting experience!
